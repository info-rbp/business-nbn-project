Eligibility & Terms

When do I receive my credit?

Credits are applied to your next billing cycle once the referred business has completed 30 days of active service on a qualifying plan.

Is there a limit to how many I can refer?

No. You can refer as many businesses as you like. If your credit balance exceeds your monthly bill, it will roll over to the following month.

*Terms and conditions apply. Referral must be a new business customer who has not held an active account with NBN Business Solutions in the last 12 months. Credit amounts are determined by the plan type selected by the referred party at the time of activation. Credits are non-transferable and cannot be exchanged for cash. NBN Business Solutions reserves the right to modify or terminate this program at any time without prior notice.
