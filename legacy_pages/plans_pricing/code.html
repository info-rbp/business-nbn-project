Frequently Asked Questions

How long does setup take? expand_more
Standard activations typically take 2-4 business days. For locations requiring new infrastructure, our team will provide a specific timeline within 24 hours of your order.
Can I bring my own modem? expand_more
Yes, you can use any NBN-compatible modem. However, for 4G backup functionality and priority support, we recommend our business-grade NBN Gateway.
What is a Static IP used for? expand_more
A Static IP is essential for hosting servers, remotely accessing office files, or running certain business applications that require a consistent address.
