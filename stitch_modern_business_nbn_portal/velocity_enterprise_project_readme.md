# Velocity Enterprise NBN Business Website

This project is a comprehensive, enterprise-grade digital experience for a Business ISP, designed to convert and support corporate and SMB clients.

## Information Architecture & Navigation

The site is structured into four primary pillars:

### 1. Growth & Conversion
- **Home Page**: High-impact entry point focused on "High-Performance NBN".
- **Plans & Pricing**: Tiered comparison of Basic, Pro, and Elite plans.
- **Dedicated Plan Pages**: Deep-dives into the Basic (100/40), Pro (250/100), and Elite (1000/1000) tiers.
- **Managed Wi-Fi & Hardware**: Showcasing premium hardware like the eero 6+ system.

### 2. Customer Acquisition (Sign-Up Flow)
- **Step 1: Address Check**: Serviceability qualification.
- **Step 2: Select Plan**: Transparent plan selection.
- **Step 3: Business Details**: Collection of ABN and authorized contact info.
- **Step 4: Review & Confirm**: Final order summary and legal acknowledgments.

### 3. Support & Service Hub
- **Support Hub**: Centralized help center with category selection.
- **Submit Ticket**: Technical support workflow with SLA response time visibility.
- **Moving Office**: Specialized coordination form for business transitions.
- **Network Status**: Real-time transparency with maintenance logs and health maps.

### 4. Brand & Compliance
- **About Us (Why Velocity?)**: Infrastructure highlights (Tier 1 network, global peering).
- **Success Stories**: Case studies (Metropol Hotel, Apex Logistics) demonstrating real-world impact.
- **SLA Details**: Legal and technical breakdown of the Business-Grade Guarantee.
- **CIS Index**: Searchable repository of Critical Information Summaries.
- **Refer a Business**: Tiered reward program for business partners.

## Interactive Capabilities

- **Cross-Linking**: All CTA buttons are logically mapped (e.g., "Select Plan" on the Home Page leads to the Sign-Up flow).
- **Navigation**: The Top Navigation bar provides instant access to all core pillars.
- **Support Workflows**: Category-specific routing ensures users find the right forms (Ticket vs. Moving Office).
- **Legal Compliance**: Regulatory links are consistently placed in the footer across all pages.

## Design System: Velocity Enterprise
- **Typography**: Hanken Grotesk (Professional, Modern).
- **Palette**: Deep Navy (Trust), Vibrant Red (Energy/Action), Pure White (Clarity).
- **Style**: High-contrast, bold layouts with generous whitespace for readability.
